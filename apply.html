Join Atlas Virtual

Think you have what it takes? Check requirements and submit your application below.

Pilot Requirements
  • 1 Minimum age — 13 years old (per IFC rules)
  • 2 Trust Level — Active member of the Infinite Flight Community (IFC)
  • 3 Flight Grade — Grade 2 or above in Infinite Flight
  • 4 Subscription — Must own a legal copy of Infinite Flight Pro
  • 5 Activity — Minimum 1 flight per month to stay active in the VA
  • 6 Must NOT be on the IFVARB Blacklist or Watchlist
What Happens Next
  • → Application reviewed by our team via IFC
  • → If accepted, receive Arambh trainee rank
  • → Complete Stage 1 theoretical exam
  • → Complete Stage 2 check-ride flight
  • → Receive full certification and operational rank
Important Note

Discord access and the Pilot Handbook are provided after you complete training. All application communication is through IFC only.
