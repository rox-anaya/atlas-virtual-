Becoming a Pilot

Training Program

Our structured two-stage certification ensures every Atlas Virtual pilot meets our professional standards before operations.

All new pilots must complete our two-stage training program before officially joining Atlas Virtual. This process begins after your application is accepted and reviewed by our team.
01
📖
Stage 1
Theoretical Entrance Exam

Complete our theoretical knowledge exam covering aviation basics, Infinite Flight procedures and Atlas Virtual standard operating procedures. Pass mark required to advance.

02
✈️
Stage 2
Practical Test and Check-Ride

Demonstrate your flying skills in a supervised check-ride conducted by one of our certified examiners. Evaluated on flight planning, procedures and aircraft handling.

✓
🏆
Certified
Atlas Virtual Airline Family

Upon passing both stages you officially join the Atlas Virtual family. Receive your certification, rank assignment, Pilot Handbook and full access to all VA operations.
