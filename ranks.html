Rank Structure

Progress through our ranks by completing flight hours and demonstrating professionalism. Each rank is represented by a unique epaulette.

Cadet — Trainee Rank
Awarded Upon Acceptance into Atlas Virtual

Every Atlas Virtual journey begins here. When your application is accepted, you receive the Cadet rank. Complete Stage 1 (theory exam) and Stage 2 (check-ride) training to earn your first operational rank and officially take to the skies as an Atlas Virtual pilot.
