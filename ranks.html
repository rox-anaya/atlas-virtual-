Progression

Rank Structure

Progress through our ranks by completing flights and demonstrating professionalism. Rank names are drawn from Sanskrit and mythology — representing strength, endurance and mastery.

🎓
Arambh — Trainee Rank
Awarded Upon Acceptance into Atlas Virtual

Arambh means "beginning" in Sanskrit. All new pilots receive this rank after their application is accepted. During Arambh, pilots complete Stage 1 and Stage 2 training before being promoted to their first operational rank.
