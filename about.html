Who We Are

About Atlas Virtual

Atlas Virtual (ATV) is a non-profit virtual airline built for the Infinite Flight simulator community. We specialise in global heavy cargo logistics and elite passenger charter operations.

We provide a world-class virtual airline experience through structured operations, professional standards, and a passionate community who share a love for realistic aviation simulation.

Our pilots are held to the highest standards in flying, conduct, discipline and professionalism. Every pilot joins through a structured training program ensuring quality operations across our global network.

When you wear the Atlas Virtual callsign, you represent something bigger than yourself — a team built on discipline, precision and brotherhood.

"I built Atlas Virtual from the ground up because I believe virtual aviation deserves the same respect as the real thing. Every pilot who joins us becomes part of something bigger. We fly with discipline, operate with precision, and grow together as a family. Welcome to Atlas Virtual."

Ravikumar
Chief Executive Officer — Atlas Virtual
